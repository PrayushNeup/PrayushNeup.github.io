GAME & WEB DESIGN

3D Immersive Environment

I developed a 3D immersive virtual environment in Unity, utilizing a first-person perspective to enhance user engagement. Low-poly models were crafted in Autodesk Maya and seamlessly integrated into Unity as interactive objects. Additionally, I composed and edited custom music and audio tracks in Adobe Audition, creating a fully immersive and dynamic virtual experience.
